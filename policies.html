Privacy & Data Protection

Federal Reserved Accounts ensures that your personal and financial information remains confidential and secure. We comply with all U.S. data privacy regulations, employing advanced encryption, restricted data access, and independent oversight to safeguard your information.

  • Your data will never be sold or disclosed without legal authorization.
  • We utilize 256-bit SSL encryption across all platform connections.
  • Users have full transparency and control over account activity logs.

Transparency & Accountability

All operations under Federal Reserved Accounts are monitored for compliance and integrity. We maintain public accountability through transparent reporting, independent auditing, and secure digital verification systems.

Information Collection

We collect minimal personal information necessary to verify identity and support secure user authentication. Data retention follows federal standards, ensuring all information is stored only as long as necessary to maintain security and compliance.

Security Practices

FRA employs advanced network monitoring, intrusion prevention, and multi-factor authentication to ensure continuous protection. Regular audits by certified federal IT teams reinforce our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of cybersecurity.
