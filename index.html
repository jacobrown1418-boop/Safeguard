Welcome to Federal Reserve Accounts

Federal Reserve Accounts provide secure, government-backed financial accounts for eligible consumers. Backed by the U.S. Treasury Department, these accounts combine strong federal oversight with modern fraud-prevention technology to keep your money and identity safe.

Our platform provides easy access to balances, statements, and account management tools while prioritizing privacy and regulatory compliance. We continually monitor for suspicious activity, perform regular audits, and offer clear steps to help you recover in case of identity theft or fraud.

Federal Reserve Accounts are designed for stability and long-term trust — giving you the confidence to manage your finances with the highest standard of governmental protection and operational excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure safe, transparent, and accessible federal financial services to protect consumers across the United States. We prioritize financial stability, fraud prevention, and rapid dispute resolution.

Working closely with the U.S. Treasury Department and other federal partners, we maintain rigorous compliance and oversight so that account holders have confidence and clear recourse when something goes wrong.

We believe in empowering consumers with education, strong protections, and easy reporting tools to keep their finances secure.

About the FTC

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) enforces consumer protection laws, investigates deceptive practices, and helps victims of fraud recover. The FTC works with other federal agencies to ensure market fairness and protect consumers from scams and identity theft.

Through enforcement, policy guidance, and public education, the FTC assists with investigations and pursues corrective actions when businesses violate consumer rights.

The FTC also publishes resources to help consumers spot phishing and scams, and it provides steps to restore credit and identity following fraud.

Security You Can Trust

Federal Reserve Accounts are protected by multi-layered security measures, including strong encryption, multi-factor authentication, and continuous transaction monitoring. These systems are regularly audited and maintained to match federal standards.

Importantly, this program is backed by the U.S. Treasury Department — providing institutional support and oversight that raises the security bar above typical commercial offerings.

Our security program includes rapid incident response, identity recovery assistance, and coordination with federal law enforcement where necessary to protect account holders.
